Letter Reveal - Reveal a letter from a word if user inputs that letter
Input: Any letter
Example:
>_ _ _ _ _
>L (Input)
>_ _ L L _
>H (Input)
>H _ L L _
>E (Input)
>H E L L _
>O (Input)
>H E L L O
Challenge: - Give the user lives, so if they guess incorrectly they lose a life.
Challenge example::
>_ _ _ _ _
>V (Input)
>"V" is not in the word! 6 lives left!
